About Us

Embed.me is an innovation-driven IT agency that lives and breathes with the motto “thinking outside the box”. We are a team that turns the innovative raw ideas of our valuable clients into something that is both aesthetically pleasing and technically very effective.
We get excited when we find a better way of doing things and are encouraged to share our knowledge with our clients every chance we get. Our sense of pride is rooted in client satisfaction and seeing about us our clients’ businesses thrive through our simple, innovative and effective product designs.

OUR VISION

By nurturing an environment of creativity, fostering innovation, and cultivating a harmonious workplace, we strive to leave a mark on the world of technology and beyond.

OUR MISSION

We excel by transforming ideas into tangible solutions. With expertise in electronics, mechanical engineering, and software development, we exceed client expectations and drive innovation.

OUR TEAM

Our team comprise of hardworking engineers having strong technical expertise in their respective fields. Our engineers are trained on-site to ensure the safety and quality of the end products.
